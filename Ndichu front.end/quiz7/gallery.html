Cats, also called domestic cats (Felis catus), are small, carnivorous (meat-eating) mammals, of the family Felidae. Domestic cats are often called house cats when kept as indoor pets. ... They are one of the most popular pets in the world. They are kept by humans for hunting rodents and companionship.
